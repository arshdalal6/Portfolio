Projects

Resume-Ify

Resume-Ify is a website that helps you find job opportunities based on your resume. You simply upload your resume and pick a location! The site reads through it to find relevant information. It then gives you a list of job applications that match your skills and experience. After you submit your resume, you'll see the results on a new page, making it an easy and personalized way to search for jobs.

UCONN Meal Planning

*NOT AFILLIATED WITH UCONN* UConn meal plan project is a web app designed to help students create personalized meal plans based on their preferences and health goals. It pulls in the latest dining hall menus and suggests meals that fit their needs, aiming to help students stay on track with their eating habits while focusing on both physical and mental well-being. The app makes it easy to stick to a routine and reach body goals with a structured eating schedule.
